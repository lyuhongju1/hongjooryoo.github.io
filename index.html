I am a physics and philosophy researcher at Johns Hopkins University (PhD), previously affiliated with UC Berkeley (Quadruple BA), Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (Postbaccalaureate Researcher), and Fermilab (Visiting Researcher). Below are my involvements:

Physics

  1. Theoretical Physics — Quantum Chromodynamics in the nonperturbative regime through new quantum computing and renormalization techniques.
  2. Experimental Physics — Dark Matter searches, hardware and analysis of Neutrino detectors like Eos and ANNIE.

Philosophy

  1. Philosophy of Explanation and Scientific Understanding — in epistemology and metaphysics.
  2. Foundations of Quantum Mechanics — bridging mathematical physics, bayesianism, and philosophy.
  3. Philosophy of Mind, AI, and Cognitive Science — exploring causation, representation, and intelligence. I also founded Hovian Inc.
