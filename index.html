As an undergraduate researcher in Physics at UC Berkeley, I am dedicated to advancing our understanding of high energy particle experimentation and lattice field theories. My academic journey is rooted in the pursuit of knowledge across multiple disciplines including physics, applied mathematics, philosophy, and cognitive science.

My current research focuses on computational studies within the Berkeley Nuclear Theory Group N3AS and the experimental work with the Orebi Gann Group at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. Each project aims to deepen our grasp of quantum phenomena and the fundamental principles governing the universe.

In addition to my research commitments, I have actively participated in the academic community through publications and presentations, striving to contribute meaningful insights to the field of physics.
