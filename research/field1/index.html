Computational LQCD Research

As part of the Berkeley Nuclear Theory Group and the Network for Neutrinos, Nuclear Astrophysics, and Symmetries (N3AS), my focus is on computational research in Lattice Quantum Chromodynamics (LQCD), investigating quantum computations of 3-body scattering amplitudes in 1+1D and refining existing computations using deformed contours and quadrature methods. This research aims to advance our understanding of quantum field theories and their implications in the physical world.
